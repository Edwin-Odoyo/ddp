About Us
Profile

Dudi Development Program is a national humanitarian, development, non-profit and non-governmental organization dedicated to improving lives of the communities through poverty alleviation, asset creation, innovations, resilience and sustainable development mechanisms in partnership with national and international partners in Kenya. The organization was established in 2018 registered with the National NGOs Board of Kenya.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to promote a world free of poverty where communities are empowered through innovations for healthy life.
Our mission is to integrate development agricultural and environmental management and conservation, access to education, water and sanitation for poverty alleviation.
